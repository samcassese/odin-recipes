Eggplant Lasagna

Image of the finished Eggplant Lasagna!

This recipe is a pretty standard lasagna recipe, except it uses eggplant in place of the pasta! Very good for people who are trying to limit their carbs.

Ingredients:

  • 1 teaspoon olive oil for brushing
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup Italian breadcrumbs
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • 2 large eggplants
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 48 ounces tomato sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Oil 2 baking sheets and a 9x13-inch baking dish with 1 teaspoon olive oil.
  2. Whisk eggs and water together in a shallow dish. Combine Parmesan cheese, bread crumbs, salt, and ground black pepper in a separate shallow dish.
  3. Dip eggplant slices into egg mixture, then press each gently into bread crumb mixture.
  4. Bake eggplant slices in the preheated oven until tops are golden brown, 20 to 25 minutes.
  5. Remove eggplant from the oven and increase temperature to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
  6. Season beef with salt and ground black pepper. Stir tomato sauce into ground beef; bring to a simmer and set sauce aside.
  7. Place 1/3 of the eggplant slices on bottom of the dish. Pour 1/3 of tomato sauce with ground beef on top of eggplant layer. Sprinkle 1/3 of mozzarella cheese on top of sauce layer.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven until cheese is melted and sauce is bubbling, 10 to 15 minutes. Cool for 5 minutes before slicing.
