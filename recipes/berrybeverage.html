Berry Beverage

Image of the finished beverage!

This recipe is extremely easy! Only one step to produce a delicious refreshment.

Ingredients:

  • 8 cups cranberry juice
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 quart vanilla ice cream, softened
  • 2 cups heavy whipping cream
  • 10 ounces pureed strawberries
  • 4 cups ginger ale
  • 1 cup sugar
  • Fresh strawberries, optional

Steps:

  1. In a large bowl or container, combine the first five ingredients; stir until smooth and the sugar is dissolved. Fold in whipped cream. Slowly add ginger ale; stir gently to mix. Pour into glasses. Garnish with strawberries if desired. Serve immediately.
